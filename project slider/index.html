Finland

Finland is the happiest country in the world.Finland has a National Day of Failure (October 13th), where Finns celebrate life setbacks as a natural path to success.

Denmark

Denmark is one of the happiest countries in the world.Denmark is home to the first amusement park in the world The Danish royal family is one of the world’s oldest.

#3

Iceland

Iceland has a high literacy rate, with over 99% of the population able to read and write.The country has a strong economy, with a high standard of living.

#4

Sweden

Sweden's official language is Swedish, but the country recognizes several minority languages, including Finnish, Yiddish, Sami, Meänkieli, and Romani.

#5

Israel

Israel is only 1/6 of 1% of the landmass of the Middle East (Israel is roughly half the size of Lake Michigan). Native-born Israelis are called Sabras.

#6

Netherlands

The Netherlands is a country unlike any other, with more than a quarter of its land below sea level. In fact, 50% of its land lies less than one meter above sea level.

#7

Norway

Norway is home to the Midnight Sun in the summer, with the sun remaining above the horizon for 24 hours in the northernmost parts of the country.
